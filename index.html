Hello World

Any nation's core values are defined by the culture and ethnicity of people living there.
Various art forms like dance, music, festivals and much more explains the uniqueness of beauty of that country.
But all these traits are suppressed under the modernism and new trends that are taking place.
Thinqart is just that one step to revive that beauty and fuse it with the modern cultures and trends.
Thinqart, being in its infancy stage, harnesses
the power of creativity of words which will attack your thinking with the
blows of beauty and inspiration. It will take time
for us to settle completely but we here provide you the
best material to read for the time being.
