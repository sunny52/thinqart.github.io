Dance

Dance is the movement of the body in a rhythmic way, usually to music and within a given space, for the
purpose of expressing an idea or emotion, releasing energy, or simply taking delight in the movement itself.
Dance is a powerful impulse, but the art of dance is that impulse channeled by skillful performers into something
that becomes intensely expressive and that may delight spectators who feel no wish to dance themselves. These
two concepts of the art of dance — dance as a powerful impulse and dance as a skillfully choreographed art; are
the two most important connecting ideas running through the subject. The transcendence of dance is also
experienced by the spectators. Drawn into the rhythms and patterns created by the dancer’s movements, they
begin to share in the emotions being expressed through them. The awareness of the body through sensations in
the joints and muscles, rather than through visual perception, not only defines the dancer’s experience of his
own body in movement but also the way in which dance exerts its power over the spectators, who not only see it
but also feel an echo of the dancer’s movements and rhythms in their own nerve endings. We can imagine what
happiness it gives to the dancers and spectators as well.

Dancers are the athletes of God.
-ALBERT EINSTEIN

It keeps those moments alive, isn't it.

Writing

Writing is a medium of human communication that represents language and emotion through
the inscription of words. The result of writing is generally called text, and the
recipient of text is called a reader, but the maker of it is known as writer. Motivations for writing
include publication, storytelling, correspondence and diary. Writing has been instrumental in
keeping history, maintaining culture, dissemination of knowledge through the media and the
formation of legal systems. It is also an important medium of expressing oneself by way of written words
as do authors, poets and the like. The most important and the most beautiful results out of
peoples' creative thinking are novels and poems. Some literary creative writers write such an
amazing stories which inspire people to adhere it. Poems are beautiful and do have
emotionally compelling purpose to infect human heart with love and enlightenment. A writer writes
to inspire many, but founds his inspiration in loneliness,
just thinking and imagining.

As a writer you ask yourself to dream while awake.
-AIMEE BENDER

Designing

Designing is brainstorming of imagination spread in colorful ideas and apply on the thing
wherever it suits. Designing often necessitates considering the aesthetic, functional, economic
and sociopolitical dimensions of both the design object and design process. It is a roadmap or a
strategic approach for someone to achieve a unique expectation. It defines the specifications, plans,
parameters, costs, activities, processes and how and what to do within legal, political, social,
environmental, safety and economic constraints in achieving that objective. Reflections on material culture
and environmental concerns guide a design philosophy. A designer have to be imaginative, always got drunk
by the wine of creative thinking. The great designs in the form of architecture, interior, graphic,
painting, handicrafts, artifacts and sculptures explains that master needs patience and creativity needs
constant appetite of inspiration and thinking. There is no better field other than designing to test
patience and power of imagination.

No master piece was ever created by a lazy artist.
-DALI

Sports

Sports are all forms of usually competitive physical activity or games which, through casual
or organised participation, aim to use, maintain or improve physical ability and skills while providing
enjoyment to participants, and in some cases, entertainment for spectators. It was man’s desire
for a healthy pastime and a method of self evaluation and competition that gave birth to sports.
Sports keeps the sense of joyment and spirit alive in players as well as spectators.
There is shouting, tense, roar, inspiration in whole over the stadium. Besides joy, sports have countless
health and physical benefits. Facts are not able to make that much mark on youngsters' minds
as sports have made. It is the rescuer from boring hours of rote learning and create boundless openness
to strive for enjoyment and play. It raise team spirit, sense to give best without caring
of result and hunger to reach the goal without giving harm to others. These are the basic skills
that a player is crafted with, spiritually.

Never say never because limits, like fears, are often just an illusion.
-MICHAEL JORDAN

Talent Affairs

Mozart playing 'Die Zauberflöte', Rukmini Devi Arundale performing 'Bharatnatyam', Tagore reciting
'Geetanjali'and Tolstoy writing 'The Kingdom of God Is Within You'; Einstein lecturing 'General Relativity',

what driven them. But not everyone in the world is as mighty and lucky to have wings to fly away from the
cage of predefined way of living life and suffer the brutality of lashes of narrow thinking. Everyone has a
passion of his own and one do figure it out in the due course of life, but question still remains what
he ends up doing with it.
